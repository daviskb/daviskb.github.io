The Portfolio of Beckett Davis

Projects

  • Metroid (Unity Remaster)
In this project, I focused primarily on the development of different enemy types, their behavior, and their interactions with the level around them, along with various other aspects such as custom level design, missiles, and a cheat mode. Used C# and Unity for development.
  • Duo
In this project, I created a puzzle platformer from scratch, focusing primarliy on the custom mechanic which allows the player to record their actions. The player can interact with this past recording, allowing for interesting puzzle solving. I focused primarily on implementing this mechanic and using the pub-sub system. Used C# and Unity for development.
  • Drawn Together
This project was a collaborative one in which we designed a 3D puzzle platformer. In this game, two players play splitscreen to solve puzzles and advance in the level. My primary role was working on level design and level mechanics, but I spent plenty of time working on things like player mechanics, 3D modeling, sound, and general bug-fixes. Used C# and Unity for development.
